About Us

Welcome to Humanitarian International

Humanitarian International is a non-profit humanitarian organization that provides support to people in need caused by either natural disasters or man-made disasters.

The organization was established in 2010 by a group of Kenyans who previously worked for international organization based in the developed world. They are from different professions. The objective of the organization is to provide support to people in need regardless of their political, religious or ehtnicity
