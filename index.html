Our Causes

Humanitarian International is guided by the following cuses.

Water and Sanitation for all

Supply for claen water for people in need through drilling boreholes, rehabilitation of water sources already in place or through water trucking

Ensuring the communities have drinkable water by chlorination of the water sources. provision of health education to the people

Ensuring good sanitation for the population in need by providing adequate latrines to the population

Provision of waste disposal services to ensure good hygiene standards are mainained in the area
